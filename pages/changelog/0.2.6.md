# 0.2.6 — 2026-07-17

## Added
- DTLS-SRTP media encryption — a third end-to-end call encryption option
  (alongside SDES-SRTP and ZRTP), matching how WebRTC-based systems and
  gateways negotiate encryption.
- Linear PCM (uncompressed) audio codec — for lab/loopback testing or gear
  that specifically wants uncompressed audio.
- Signed Windows installer (`.msi`).

## Fixed
- Windows: DeeLip could fail to launch with no window or tray icon, and
  could hang at startup while registering an account in the background.
- Direct calls between two DeeLip instances with no SIP server in between
  could get stuck — hanging up, holding, or transferring silently failed
  to reach the other side.
- Video calls could show corrupted or frozen frames when network packets
  arrived out of order.
- G.729 could be added in Settings but was never actually offered on a call.
- An account could get stuck retrying registration forever after being
  permanently rejected, instead of giving up.
- Calls could hang indefinitely when relaying media through a TURN server
  under some network conditions.
- Contacts: editing the wrong contact after deleting one above it in the list.

## Changed
- Faster Contacts search and app startup.
